5. Storage & Handling Guidelines
Storage Temperature
Store below 35°C. Avoid direct sunlight on wrapping.
Shelf Life (Sealed)
12 months from harvest date under proper storage conditions.
After Opening
Use within 2–3 days. Reseal cut face tightly after each feeding.
Wrapping Damage
Inspect for punctures before use. Patch immediately with silage repair tape.
Feeding Area
Remove uneaten silage within 4 hours in hot weather (> 30°C).
Signs of Spoilage
Discard if black/grey colour, butyric odour, or extensive mould growth.
